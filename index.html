Kwame Nkrumah

The first Prime Minister and President of Ghana.

Here's a time line of Kwame Nkrumah's life:

  • 1909 - Born in Nkroful,Gold Coast(now Ghana)

  • 1935 - Leaves his family to pursue further studies in the United States.He entered Lincoln University in Pennsylvania

  • 1939 - After graduation he obtained master's degrees from Lincoln and from the University of Pennsylvania

  • 1945 - He left the United States and went to England where he organised the 5th Pan-African Congress in Manchester.

  • 1947 - He returned home. He became the general secretary where he addressed meetings throughout the Gold Coast and began to create a mass base for the new movement

  • 1948 - The British breifly arrested Nkrumah and other leaders of the UGCC

  • 1949 - He formed the new Convention Peoples Party(CPP) a mass-based party that was commited to a program of immediate self government.

  • 1950 - He initiated a campaign of "positive action," involving nonviolent protests, strikes, and noncooperation with the British colonial authorities

  • 1951 - He was arrested and sentence to one year's imprisonment, But the Gold Coast's first general election demonstrated the support the CPP had already won.

  • 1952 - He was elected to parliament. He was released from prison to become leader of government business and prime minsiter of the Gold Coast

"Those who would judge us merely by the heights we have achieved would do well to remember the depths from which we started."
-Kwame Nkrumah, having led the Gold Coast to independence from the Britian in 1957.
